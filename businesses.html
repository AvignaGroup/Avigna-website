Tailored Solutions for Every Business

We understand the unique demands of your industry and provide specialized infrastructure to drive your success.

Partnering with Industry Leaders

Avigna's customer-centric approach means we go beyond providing space. We deliver integrated solutions that enhance operational efficiency, ensure supply chain resilience, and support the growth of our diverse client base across India's most vital economic sectors.

E-Commerce & Retail

Our parks are strategically located near major consumption centers, enabling faster last-mile delivery. We offer scalable spaces with optimized layouts for efficient sorting, storage, and fulfillment operations.

Third-Party Logistics (3PL)

We provide flexible, multi-user facilities with high-grade specifications that allow 3PL providers to optimize their distribution networks and serve a wide range of clients with maximum efficiency and reliability.

Manufacturing & Automotive

Our facilities feature high-strength floors, ample power supply, and robust infrastructure to support light manufacturing and assembly lines, helping streamline production and supply chain logistics.

FMCG

We offer Grade-A compliant warehouses that meet the stringent hygiene and quality standards of the FMCG sector, ensuring product integrity from storage to distribution.

Cold Storage

Our parks are equipped to support the development of temperature-controlled and cold storage facilities, vital for the pharmaceutical, agricultural, and processed food industries.

Engineering

We provide robust infrastructure capable of handling heavy machinery and components, supporting the storage and logistics needs of the heavy engineering sector.
